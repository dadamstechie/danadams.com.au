Ten commandments of the rental.

Handed down not from a mountain, but from our very tired front-desk staff.

Rule I

Thou shalt return the car.

Rule II

Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's parking spot — there is plenty for everyone, we promise.

Rule III

If thou crasheth the car, thou shalt pay any insurance premiums that result. We forgive, but the insurer does not. 😂

Rule IV

Honour thy return time, for late fees are the wages of procrastination.

Rule V

Thou shalt not smoke within these walls — the only thing that should be smoking is our tyres, and even then, ideally not.

Rule VI

Blessed are the designated drivers, for they shall inherit the good parking karma.

Questions & answers

Frequently asked (and forgiven).

What's actually included in the $40 a day?

The car, unlimited local kilometres, a full tank at pickup, and 24/7 roadside support. What's not included: your driving skills, which remain entirely your own responsibility.

What happens if I crash it?

First, we hope you're okay. Second — genuinely, no joke here — you're responsible for any insurance premiums that result from the incident. We said it in the pricing section too, because it bears repeating (and because it made us laugh). 😂

Do you offer forgiveness for late returns?

Seventy times seven, technically. In practice, a $25 late fee kicks in after a two-hour grace period. Grace has limits; the fee does not.

Can I rent on a Sunday?

Absolutely — we're open seven days a week. We just ask that you drive a little more carefully than usual, out of respect for the day and for our insurance rates.

Is there a minimum age to rent?

You need to be 21 or older with a valid driver's licence. No commandments about age in the Bible, but our insurer had a few thoughts.
