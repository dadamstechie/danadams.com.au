About

Engineering where
industry meets purpose.

I'm Dan, a third-year engineering student at the University of Wollongong with a focus on sustainable design and infrastructure systems. Alongside my degree, I've spent two years as an Engineering Cadet at BlueScope Steel — working on real manufacturing challenges and translating academic theory into industrial practice.

My interests sit at the intersection of infrastructure, sustainability, and emerging technology. I'm drawn to problems that are both technically complex and socially meaningful — from aged-care facility design in regional New Zealand to national security strategy in an AI-enabled world.

Outside of engineering, I volunteer in the community and hold an active interest in ASX investment analysis and defence policy.
